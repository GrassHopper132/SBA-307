Power

Power Book III: Raising Kanan is a 1990s-set spin-off of the original Power series. It chronicles the origin story of Kanan Stark (played by Mekai Curtis) and his violent coming-of-age as a teenager entering the drug trade under the guidance of his ambitious mother, Raquel "Raq" Thomas.

Fun Fact: The theme song for Raising Kanan, "Part of the Game," was performed by 50 Cent!

Notable Characters

  • Kanan Stark
  • Raquel "Raq" Thomas
  • LaVerne "Juke" Thomas

Top Story Arcs

  1. Kanan's initiation into the family business.
  2. Raq's turf war with rival syndicates.
  3. Jukebox's personal struggles and musical aspirations.
